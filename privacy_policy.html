We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless you choose to contact us directly. However, we may automatically collect limited, non-personal data such as:

Our site may use cookies or analytics tools like Google Analytics. These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing
